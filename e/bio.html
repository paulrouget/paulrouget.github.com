Paul Rouget's Bio

Paul Rouget is a Firefox engineer and a technology evangelist at Mozilla. He is instrumental in combining the latest and greatest open Web technologies. His goal is to push the boundaries of the Web and ensure that it continues to develop into an open and accessible platform for all. He has been involved with the Mozilla project since 2003 both as a volunteer and as staff.
