Bio

Paul Rouget is a technology evangelist at Mozilla and is instrumental in combining the latest and greatest open Web technologies. His goal is to push the boundaries of the Web and ensure that it continues to develop into an open and accessible platform for all. Paul works closely with the platform team at Mozilla to explore how new technologies can continue to improve Firefox. Paul creates beautiful demos using HTML5 to illustrating where the Web can go. He is also behind hacks.mozilla.org and @mozhacks. He has been involved with the Mozilla project since 2003 both as a volunteer and as staff.
