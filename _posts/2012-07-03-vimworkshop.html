Atelier Vim, Paris, le 20 Juillet

Drew Neil (@nelstrom) connu pour vimcast.org animera un atelier Vim à Paris le 20 Juillet (en anglais).

Il y a 2 sessions:

  • matin: http://paris-vimcasts-am-1.eventbrite.com/
  • après-midi: http://paris-vimcasts-pm-1.eventbrite.com/

Drew: My workshops last 3 hours, and they cover some of the most important features of Vim. I'll show you how to make Vim's modes work for you, rather than against you. We'll spend time studying Vim's most important feature: the dot command, which lets you repeat your last change. Once you've learned the secrets of the dot command, you'll be able to complete repetitive tasks effortlessly.

Drew est aussi l'auteur de "Practical Vim". Vu la qualité du bouquin, je ne saurais que trop recommender son atelier.
