CSS Transforms and IndexedDB unprefixed in Firefox.

"Good news everyone!"

We have unprefixed CSS transforms and and IndexedDB in Firefox Nightly (Firefox 16). -moz-transform becomes transform and window.mozIndexedDB becomes window.indexedDB.

Related bugs:

  • Bug 726378 - Unprefix IndexedDB
  • Bug 745523 - [css3-transforms] Unprefix transforms

PS: -moz-tranform is still supported. But it will probably be removed. See bug 770560.
